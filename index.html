About Me

Steven Quast was born February 12, 1990. He graduated in 2008 from Clear Brook High School before joining the United States Navy in January 2009. In January 2014, he left the service. Using the post 9/11 GI Bill, Steven Quast entered Full Sail University. He finished the schooling as Salutatorian with a final G.P.A. of 3.54, earning a Bachelor of Science in Game Development. From there, Steven decided to continue learning different programming languages and decided to join a Coding Bootcamp powered by UT. He hopes to do well in the course and prove that he is a skilled individual worth hiring.
